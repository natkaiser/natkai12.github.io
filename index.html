Omniscient Readers Viewpoint

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint (ORV) is a story about a 28-year-old office worker named Kim Dokja who is the sole reader of a web novel about an apocalypse. The novel's story follows Yoo Joonghyuk, who can return to the beginning of the apocalypse whenever he dies. Dokja finds comfort in the novel's story, which shows that the protagonist suffered but kept going. It's a pretty good novel, an 9/10 from me.
